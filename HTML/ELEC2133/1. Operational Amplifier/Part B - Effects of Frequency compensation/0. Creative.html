Instructions and Simulation Notes

Creative mode allows you to construct, simulate and implement a circuit in any way within the constraints of the hardware. You have been provided with every node necessary for constructing the circuit. Joining nodes will produce an error.

When this lab is opened, the relay controlling the power supply (Power - Port 1 Line 3 - ON) will switch ON. Once your circuit has been constructed, it will be checked against a set of possible valid implementations. If your circuit matches, it will be implemented on the hardware
